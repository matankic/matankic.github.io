Matan Kichler

I am a M.Sc student in the Department of Computer Science and Applied Mathematics at the Weizmann Institute of Science. Prior to that, I completed my B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics (summa cum laude) and B.Sc. in Computer Science (summa cum laude) at Holon Institute of Technology (HIT).

My primary research interests are in computational imaging and the development of deep learning methods for computer vision. I’m currently working in Prof. Mark Sheinin’s lab, where we use laser-speckle vibrometry to sense object vibrations and apply those measurements to solve a variety of problems.
